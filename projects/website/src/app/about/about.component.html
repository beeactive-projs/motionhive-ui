Our story

Built for people who show up.

MotionHive exists because staying active together shouldn't require five different apps.

The problem

Why this exists?

If you've ever organised a group activity — a class, a run, a weekly session — you know how it goes. WhatsApp for scheduling, spreadsheets for attendance, Instagram for updates, and half the group still out of the loop.

There's no single place where the person leading and the people showing up can just stay in sync. No noise, no switching between apps, no chaos.

That's what MotionHive is. One platform for active communities — whether you're running a yoga class, a hiking group, a martial arts club, or anything that gets people moving together.

It's early and we're building it in the open. No inflated numbers, no empty promises. Just the thing that should've existed a long time ago.
